## Phrases to never use in Amara captions

"unlock your potential"
"hustle hard"
"no excuses"
"trust the process"
"this is your sign"
"obsessed with"
"all the things"
"living my best life"
"main character energy"
"the algorithm gods"
"that girl"
"clean girl"

These are all overused, hustle-coded, or read as content-creator-speak rather than someone actually living the protocols.
